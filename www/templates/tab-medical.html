For on base emergencies call 911 and tell the operator you are on Dyess Air Force Base

Emergency 911

Lost Child/Parent. The Lost Child/Parent station is in Hangar 4315. There will be signs to point to this location as people walk from the ECP. There will be an optional child registration table at the ECP where parents can get stamped/wristbands.

First Aid Station. This station is also in Hangar 4315. For minor cuts, scraps, burns, and for coordinating emergency medical services. MetroCare and AirEvac may also be located near here.

Roving Medics. We will have roving medics on golf carts with red cross arm bands. They can drive people to the First Aid Station if you need assistance. They can also communicate directly with the First Aid Stations to coordinate emergency medical services, and start the process for a Lost Child situation.
